Add and edit Type just as you would in a text editor or word processor. Press enter to add new items.
Show controls Hover over the bullet of an item.
Zoom Zoom in: Click the bullet of an item.
(Shortcut: altcontrol + right ( or > ))

Zoom out: Click on the WorkFlowy logo to fully zoom out. Click on individual ancestor items to zoom to them.
(Shortcut: altcontrol + left ( or < ))
Expand / collapse Click the '+' or '-' that appears when you hover over an item. Double click it to expand all. To expand all of the zoomed item, double click its name. Double click the WorkFlowy logo to expand all your lists.
(Shortcut: control+space)
Change indent Increase indent: tab

Decrease indent: shift+tab
Move Drag the move icon that appears to the right when you hover over an item.
(Shortcut: altcontrol+shift+arrow keys)
Add a note Click "Add Note" in the item controls.
(Shortcut: shift+enter)
Complete Click "Complete" in the item controls.
(Shortcut: control+enter)
Delete Click "Delete" in the item controls.
(Shortcut: control+shift+backspace)
Search Type in the search box that's in the top bar. Click the "x" that appears in the box to exit search mode.
(Shortcut: escape)
Show/hide completed items Click the button in the top bar.
(Shortcut: control+o)
Star and switch pages Click the star in the top right of the page to star the current page.
(Shortcut: control+shift+∗)

Click the starred-page button in the top bar to show the list of starred pages. Click one to switch.
(Shortcut: control+;) (Hold control while repeatedly pressing ';' to select a page.)
Bold / italic Highlight text and use shortcuts below to toggle formatting, or use shortcuts while typing.
(Shortcuts: control+b (bold), control+i (italic) )
Share and Collaborate Click "Share" in the item controls to share or collaborate over a sublist with others.
Duplicate Click "Duplicate" in the item controls to duplicate a list. The duplicate copy will appear immediately below the original.
Export Click "Export" in the item controls, then copy the text of your list to another program of your choice. To export all of your lists, click "Export All" in the top-right menu.

Frequently Asked Questions

What features are coming up for WorkFlowy?

The following are some of the features we plan on building into WorkFlowy. We'll implement them with the standard WorkFlowy style and simplicity.

  • Offline access
  • Dates & reminders
  • Multi-item selection
  • Advanced tagging options
  • Collaboration improvements
  • API

How do I access WorkFlowy on my mobile device?

We have an iOS app that works on iPhone and iPad. The Android app is still a little ways off.

In the meantime, navigate to workflowy.com in your phone's browser. We currently support iPhone (and iPad) as well as Android phones. We want to support all mobile devices eventually (including Blackberry, Windows Phone, etc.).

Can I have multiple WorkFlowy documents?

Every item in a WorkFlowy list is also a document. Click on the bullet point for an item to zoom in and treat that item like a document. You can then edit and share it independently. Make an item on your home screen for each item you want to be its own document, then click on its bullet when you want to work on it. We're planning on adding a bookmark feature that makes it easier to keep track of your most important lists inside WorkFlowy.

How secure is my account?

All your WorkFlowy data is stored on our servers, which means you can access it anywhere, from any computer. We have taken industry-standard measures to secure the data, and we make secure backups daily.

Why don't the keyboard shortcuts work in new versions of Mac OS X?

The most recent version of Mac OS X has default shortcuts for ExposÃ© and Spaces that conflict with WorkFlowy's Move and Zoom shortcuts. We're working on this issue, but for now you'll have to change the Mac defaults if you want to use the WorkFlowy shortcuts. (You do this by going to System Preferences > Keyboard > Keyboard Shortcuts > Mission Control and unchecking the options for 1) "Mission Control" 2) "Show desktop" 3) "Move left a space" and 4) "Move right a space").

Please bear with us - it's tricky to come up with shortcuts that don't conflict with anything.

How do I print?

Just use your browser's normal Print feature (generally File -> Print). This will print a nicely-formatted version of whatever you're currently viewing in WorkFlowy.
